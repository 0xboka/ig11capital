Investment Mandate

Delta-Neutral Stablecoin Yield at Institutional Scale

IG11 Capital is a Mauritius-based hedge fund dedicated to generating consistent, risk-adjusted USD yield through a diversified portfolio of stablecoin strategies — with zero exposure to directional crypto market risk.

IG11 deploys institutional capital exclusively within the stablecoin universe, using blue-chip DeFi primitives: money markets, fixed-rate protocols, DEX liquidity and governance incentives. All strategies are deployed through Gami Vaults, institutional-grade infrastructure backed by Fireblocks and Warwyck Private Bank custody.

IG11 Capital is the delta-neutral arm of Mirabeau Capital, designed to serve institutional investors, family offices and qualified private investors seeking USD-denominated yield without exposure to crypto market volatility.

USD Share Class — Built on fully regulated USD stablecoins, targeting 20%+ absolute returns in USD terms. Capital is highly liquid with a 15-day redemption notice and monthly redemption windows.

Since inception in January 2025, IG11 Capital has delivered 28% APY — outperforming its 20% target through organic DeFi strategies and exclusive proprietary deals negotiated directly with on-chain protocols.

Mission

Why IG11 Capital?

Notre mission est d'offrir des stratégies d'investissement
pouvant s'adapter à toutes les conditions de marché
et de générer des rendements composés élevés pour nos clients.

Decentralized Finance (DeFi) offers competitive and sustainable yield through transparent, on-chain mechanisms. However, institutional adoption has been limited by perceived complexity, custody constraints, and the volatility of underlying crypto assets.

IG11 Capital provides a market-neutral approach — generating yield fully independent of digital asset market fluctuations. By focusing exclusively on stablecoin strategies, IG11 eliminates directional risk while capturing the structural inefficiencies and governance incentives native to DeFi protocols.

As more institutional investors recognize stablecoins as a legitimate cash management instrument, IG11 Capital bridges the gap: delivering DeFi-native yield within a fully regulated Mauritius fund structure, with full USD-based accounting and monthly liquidity — accessible to family offices, qualified investors, and institutional treasuries.
